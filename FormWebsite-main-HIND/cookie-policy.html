Our Cookie Policy: Unleashing the Trackers

Welcome! You're probably here because a banner vaguely mentioned "cookies." Let's be honest: they aren't delicious baked goods. They're tiny digital surveillance tools, and we use them enthusiastically.

1. What ARE Cookies, Really?

Think of cookies as digital tags we slap onto your browser the moment you arrive. They help us recognize you when you come back, track where you go on our site (and often, elsewhere on the web), and generally build a profile so detailed, it'd make a spy blush. All in the name of "enhancing user experience," of course. Our experience, mostly.

2. The Types of Cookies We Deploy (Our Arsenal)

We utilize a sophisticated array of cookies for maximum data extraction:

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: Necessary for us to track you effectively. Without these, our business model collapses. So, yeah, pretty necessary.
  • Performance & Analytics Cookies: These watch how you click, scroll, and hesitate. We use this data to judge your decision-making skills and optimize our data selling strategies.
  • Functionality Cookies: Remembers your login info so we know exactly which user's data we're auctioning off this week. Convenient!
  • Targeting & Advertising Cookies: The crown jewels! These build that juicy profile used by us and our partners-in-crime trusted third parties to show you ads for things you *might* buy, or things you looked at once three years ago.
  • Super-Mega-Persistent Cookiesâ„¢: These bad boys are designed to survive browser clearing, incognito mode, and potentially small EMP blasts. We're quite proud of them.
  • Experimental Mind-Reading Cookies (Beta): We're testing these. If you suddenly crave a specific brand of pickles, it might be working. No promises.

3. Third-Party Cookies: It's a Data Party!

We don't hoard all the fun! We generously allow various third parties (ad networks, analytics firms, random entities who pay us) to plant their own cookies through our site. It's a delightful ecosystem where everyone gets a piece of your digital footprint. Your data's popularity is astounding!

4. Managing Your Cookies (A Noble, Yet Futile Gesture)

Your browser likely offers settings to "manage" or "block" cookies. Feel free to click those buttons! It's a great way to feel like you have control. While you're doing that, we're probably deploying newer, sneakier tracking methods anyway. But hey, A for effort!

Rejecting cookies might "break" parts of our site. By "break," we mean slightly inconvenience our tracking. We strongly advise against it for your own... uh... seamless browsing experience.

5. Consent (You Already Gave It)

By continuing to use Navigui after seeing our friendly cookie banner (or even if you didn't see it, we assume you psychically knew), you consent to our full, unbridled use of cookies as described above. Thanks for playing!
